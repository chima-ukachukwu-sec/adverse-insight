EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

1. Compensation. Employee shall receive a salary of $85,000 per year, payable bi-weekly.

2. Non-Compete Clause. Employee agrees not to work for any competitor within a 200-mile radius for a period of 5 years after termination of employment, regardless of the reason for termination.

3. Intellectual Property. All inventions, discoveries, and work product created by Employee during the term of employment, whether during work hours or on Employee's own time, shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

4. Termination. The Company may terminate Employee at any time, with or without cause, upon 24 hours written notice. Employee may only terminate employment upon 90 days written notice and must complete all assigned projects before departure.

5. Data Rights. Employee grants Company the right to collect, store, and share Employee's personal data, including biometric data collected from office security systems, with any third-party partner at Company's sole discretion.

6. Arbitration. Any dispute arising from this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in the state of Delaware, with each party bearing their own legal costs regardless of outcome.
